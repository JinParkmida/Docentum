# Code of Conduct

We are committed to fostering a welcoming and inclusive environment for everyone who engages with **Docentum** — as users, contributors, and collaborators.

---

## 🤝 Our Pledge

In the interest of promoting a safe and respectful community, we pledge to:

- Use welcoming and inclusive language
- Respect differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Show empathy toward other participants

---

## 🚫 Unacceptable Behavior

We will not tolerate:

- Harassment, abuse, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory jokes, slurs, or exclusionary behavior
- Sustained disruptive conduct that hinders collaboration
- Inappropriate sexual attention or language

---

## 🛠 Enforcement

Violations of the Code of Conduct may be reported by contacting the project maintainer at:

📬 [jiniusme@pm.me](mailto:jiniusme@pm.me)

All reports will be reviewed with confidentiality and handled respectfully.

---

## 🌍 Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including:

- GitHub repositories
- Issue trackers
- Pull requests
- Discussions and comments
- Community interactions (e.g. Discord, Zoom, etc.)
